💡 About

I am a materials researcher passionate about developing high-performance carbon fiber composite materials for structural and textile applications. My doctoral research at IIT Roorkee laid the groundwork for understanding the microstructural evolution, mechanical behavior, and failure mechanisms of CFRPs. I now bring that expertise to the Textile and Fibre Engineering department at IIT Delhi, exploring next-generation fiber architectures and their industrial applications. I also write about materials science, share research insights, and occasionally post video lectures on composite manufacturing.
